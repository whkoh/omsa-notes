1. Module 14: Forecasting (Demand)

1.1. M14L01: Forecasting

1.1.1. What's forecasting

Prediction of future events for planning.

Uses:

Strategic planning
long-term capacity
Finance and accounting
budgeting, cost control
Marketing
Future sales trends, NPI
Production and operations
staffing, supplier relations

1.1.2. Forecast characteristics

  • Almost always wrong
  • More accurate at aggregate level (errors offset)
  • More accurate at short time horizon
  • Should include error estimate/ranges
  • Does not sub for actual demand

1.1.3. Patterns of demand

  1. Trend
  2. Seasonality
  3. Cyclic elements
  4. Autocorrelation
  5. Random variation

1.1.4. Multiple patterns

Are possible. e.g. seasonal variation can exist within a wider linear trend

1.1.5. Questions

  • Why forecast?
  • What systems will consume the forecast?
  • How important is the past in predicting future?

Answers will determine time horizons, techniques, LOD.
